Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026

Audio News Daily ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any information related to your use of our application. This Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding data collection and usage.

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

Audio News Daily is a news listening application. We are committed to maintaining the trust and confidence of our users. We do not sell, rent, or trade any user information to third parties. We do not build advertising profiles. We do not track your behaviour across other applications or websites.

2. Information We Collect

Audio News Daily collects only the minimum information necessary to provide you with a functional and personalised experience. We do not require you to create an account, provide your name, email address, or any other personal identification to use our app.

The only information collected is your device's notification token, which is used solely for the purpose of delivering news update notifications to your device. This token is not linked to your identity in any way.

Your country and language preferences are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

3. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used exclusively to deliver timely news notifications to your device. It is never used for marketing, advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the core functionality of the application.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. All data stored on our servers is protected with industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections and access controls. Only authorised systems can access stored notification tokens, and they are used solely to deliver notifications.

5. Children's Privacy

Audio News Daily is a general news application intended for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take immediate steps to delete such information.

6. News Content

The news content provided in Audio News Daily is sourced from publicly available news sources and rewritten for audio delivery. We do not store any information about which articles you read or listen to. Your reading and listening history remains entirely on your device.

7. Notifications

Audio News Daily may send push notifications to inform you of new news updates and your daily morning briefing. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings. Disabling notifications will not affect your ability to use the app.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal and regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the application after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of any information we hold related to your device. Since we collect no personal information, there is nothing to access, correct, or export. If you wish to remove your notification token from our systems, simply uninstall the application.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026

Audio News Daily ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our service:

Device Token: We collect your device's push notification token solely to send you news update notifications. This token is stored securely and is not linked to any personal identity.

Country Preference: Your selected country is stored locally on your device only. We do not transmit this to our servers.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect or store any of the following:

— Your name, email address, or phone number

— Your location or GPS data

— Your browsing history or reading habits

— Any payment or financial information

— Any personal identification information

3. How We Use Your Information

Your device token is used solely to deliver push notifications about new news updates. We do not use it for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

Supabase: Used as our backend database to store and serve news articles and morning briefings. No personal data is stored in Supabase.

Groq AI: Used to rewrite and summarise news articles into audio-friendly scripts. No personal data is shared with Groq.

Expo Notifications: Used to deliver push notifications to your device.

CricAPI: Used to fetch live cricket scores. No personal data is shared.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your device token from unauthorised access. We use Row Level Security (RLS) on our database to ensure device tokens are only accessible by our secure server functions.

6. Children's Privacy

Audio News Daily is a general news application suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

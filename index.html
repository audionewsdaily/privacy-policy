Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026

Last Updated: April 12, 2026

Welcome to Audio News Daily. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains clearly and honestly how we handle your information when you use our mobile application. Please read it carefully.

1. About Audio News Daily

Audio News Daily is a free audio news application available on Android. The app delivers AI-powered audio news reports, daily morning briefings, live cricket scores, and financial market updates across five countries — India, United States, United Kingdom, Canada, and Australia. The app is completely free to use and requires no account, registration, or login of any kind.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Audio News Daily is built with your privacy as a priority. We do not collect the following:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any contact details
  • Your precise or approximate location or GPS data
  • Your device's contacts, photos, camera, or microphone
  • Your browsing history, search history, or reading and listening habits
  • Any financial or payment information
  • Any personal identification information of any kind

You do not need to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal details to use Audio News Daily.

3. Information We Collect

Audio News Daily collects only the absolute minimum information necessary to provide core functionality of the app.

Push Notifications: If you choose to enable push notifications, your device generates a unique identifier used solely to deliver news update notifications to your device. This identifier is not linked to your personal identity in any way and is never used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than delivering notifications you have opted into.

Country Preference: The country you select in the app is stored locally on your device only. This information never leaves your device and is never transmitted or shared with anyone.

4. How We Use Your Information

The only information we handle is your optional notification identifier, which is used solely to send you timely notifications about new news reports and your daily morning briefing. It is never used for any other purpose, including marketing, advertising, analytics, or user profiling.

5. Data Sharing

Audio News Daily does not sell, rent, trade, lease, or share your data with any third party, advertiser, data broker, or analytics company under any circumstances. Your information belongs to you alone.

Our app relies on secure, industry-standard backend services to deliver news content, push notifications, and live data. None of these services receive any personal user data from you.

6. We Do Not Track You

Audio News Daily does not use any tracking technologies of any kind. This includes:

  • No cookies or browser tracking
  • No analytics or crash reporting tools that collect personal data
  • No advertising networks or ad trackers
  • No cross-app or cross-website tracking
  • No user profiling or behavioural analysis

Your activity within the app — what you listen to, how long you use it, which categories you browse — is never monitored, recorded, or shared with anyone.

7. Advertising

Audio News Daily currently does not display any advertisements. The app is completely free and ad-free. If advertising is introduced in any future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly and users will be informed in advance.

8. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. All data handled by Audio News Daily is protected using industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections and strict access controls. Only authorised systems can access stored notification identifiers, and they are used solely to deliver the notifications you have requested.

If you uninstall the app, your notification identifier becomes inactive and can no longer be used to contact your device.

9. Children's Privacy

Audio News Daily is a general news application suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13 or from any user. Since we collect no personal data, there is no risk to children's privacy. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our app, please contact us and we will take immediate steps to address it.

10. Your Rights

Since Audio News Daily collects no personal data beyond an optional notification identifier, your privacy is inherently protected. However, you have the following options at any time:

  • Disable Notifications: You can turn off push notifications at any time through your device's Settings app.
  • Delete Your Data: Simply uninstalling the app removes your notification identifier from active use. You may also contact us to request explicit deletion.
  • Change Country Preference: Since your country preference is stored only on your device, you can change it at any time within the app settings.

11. News Content

All news reports on Audio News Daily are original AI-powered content created for informational purposes only. We use publicly available news topics as inspiration but do not reproduce, copy, or store any original third-party content. Our content does not involve any processing of user data.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will update the Last Updated date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of Audio News Daily after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

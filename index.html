Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026

Last Updated: April 12, 2026

Welcome to Audio News Daily. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains clearly and honestly how we handle your information when you use our mobile application. Please read it carefully.

1. About Audio News Daily

Audio News Daily is a free audio news application available on Android. The app delivers AI-generated audio news reports, daily morning briefings, live cricket scores, and market updates across five countries — India, United States, United Kingdom, Canada, and Australia. The app is free to use and requires no account or registration of any kind.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Audio News Daily is built with your privacy as a priority. We do not collect the following:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any contact details
  • Your precise or approximate location or GPS data
  • Your device's contacts, photos, camera, or microphone
  • Your browsing history, search history, or reading habits
  • Any financial or payment information
  • Any personal identification information of any kind

You do not need to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal details to use Audio News Daily.

3. Information We Collect

Audio News Daily collects only the absolute minimum information necessary to provide core functionality:

Push Notification Token: If you choose to enable push notifications, your device generates a unique notification token. This token is stored securely on our servers and is used only to deliver news update notifications to your device. It is not linked to your identity in any way and is never used for advertising or profiling.

Country Preference: The country you select in the app is stored locally on your device using your device's storage. This information never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

4. How We Use Your Information

The only piece of information we store — your push notification token — is used solely for one purpose: to send you timely notifications about new news reports and your daily morning briefing. It is never used for any other purpose, including marketing, advertising, analytics, or profiling.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

Audio News Daily does not sell, rent, trade, lease, or share your data with any third party, advertiser, data broker, or analytics company under any circumstances.

Our app uses the following backend services to operate:

  • Supabase: A secure backend database used to store and serve news articles and morning briefings. No personal user data is stored in Supabase.
  • Expo Notifications: A service used to deliver push notifications to your device via your device's notification system.
  • CricAPI: A cricket data API used to display live match scores. No user data is shared with this service.
  • Yahoo Finance: Used to fetch stock market index data. No user data is shared.

Each of these services is used solely for the technical operation of the app. None of them receive any personal user data.

6. We Do Not Track You

Audio News Daily does not use any tracking technologies of any kind. This includes:

  • No cookies or browser tracking
  • No analytics or crash reporting tools
  • No advertising networks or ad trackers
  • No cross-app or cross-website tracking
  • No user profiling or behavioural analysis

Your activity within the app — what you listen to, how long you use it, which categories you browse — is never monitored, recorded, or shared.

7. Advertising

Audio News Daily currently does not display any advertisements. The app is completely free and ad-free. If advertising is introduced in future versions, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly and users will be notified.

8. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. The only data we store — push notification tokens — is protected using industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and database-level access controls. Only authorised server functions can access stored tokens, and they are used solely to deliver notifications.

We retain notification tokens only as long as they are needed to provide the notification service. If you uninstall the app, your token becomes inactive and can no longer be used.

9. Children's Privacy

Audio News Daily is a general news application suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13 or from any user. Since we collect no personal data at all, there is no risk to children's privacy. If you believe a child has somehow provided personal information through our app, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to address it.

10. Your Rights

Since Audio News Daily collects no personal data beyond an optional notification token, your privacy rights are inherently protected. However, you have the following options:

  • Disable Notifications: You can turn off push notifications at any time through your device's Settings app, which will stop any further use of your notification token.
  • Delete Your Data: Simply uninstalling the app removes your notification token from active use. You may also contact us to request explicit deletion of your token from our servers.
  • Change Country Preference: Since your country preference is stored only on your device, you can change or clear it at any time within the app.

11. News Content and AI

All news reports on Audio News Daily are original AI-generated content created for informational purposes. We use publicly available news topics as inspiration but do not reproduce, copy, or store any original source material within our systems. Our content is generated fresh and does not involve any processing of user data.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of Audio News Daily after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
